Leave Guidelines
Leave Policy Information
  • Sick Leave (SL): Up to 12 days per year for medical reasons
  • Casual Leave (CL): Up to 10 days per year for personal matters
  • Earned Leave (EL): 1 day for every 20 days of work
  • Maternity Leave (ML): 180 days for female employees
  • Paternity Leave (PL): 15 days for male employees (child birth)
Important Notes

All leave requests must be submitted at least 5 days in advance
Leave requests will be reviewed by your manager
